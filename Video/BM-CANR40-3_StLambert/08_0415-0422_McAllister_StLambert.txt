Lance Sergeant Earl Henry McAllister of Hamilton Ontario. Killed in action in Belgium trying to save the life of a comrade. His exploits in Saint Lambert earned him fame in Hamilton. The claim of number of prisoners varies only a little: 150 according to CANR 40.3 and 160 in newspapers.
