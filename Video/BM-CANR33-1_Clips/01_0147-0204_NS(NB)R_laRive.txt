North Shore (New Brunswick) Regiment landing in front of the house known as les Hirondelles, at la Rive between Bernières-sur-Mer and Saint-Aubin-sur-Mer. This is the only footage of the initial assault on D-day and was some of the first footage seen around the world. The man seen in profile has been claimed to be Private George Baker of A Company. The original footage is three rolls of one minute each from three different LCAs.
